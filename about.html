About Me

I am a positive self-starter and a true team player who thrives on collaborating with others throughout the UX design process. What really sparks my fire is the user-centric approach, from discovery/research and empathising with the user to how it ties into brand and strategy, in order to make solid design decisions.

As a highly motivated individual I come to the UX design field from a media production background where I have had the opportunity to expand my skills beyond just production and the creative. While leading the conceptualising, development, writing and presenting of countless marketing, broadcast and social media projects I have embraced the ethos that content development and storytelling is all about solving problems to create engaging experiences.

Having seen the benefits of audience research, ideation and viewer testing in the creative field is what, over the past year, have allowed me to immerse myself in all aspects of the UX process; from research, personas and user journeys to wireframing, prototyping, testing and final design deliverables. I believe my history and drive sets me apart to contribute to the UX process.
