ABOUT ME

I am a highly motivated individual who comes to the UX Design field from a content production background where I have had the opportunity to build and work within agile teams to deliver countless multi-platform projects, from marketing and digital to broadcast and social media. In addition to content, part of my previous experience have involved working with digital teams on websites, CMS, apps, media players and more.

Having been a champion and seeing the benefits of research, testing and the iterative process is what ultimately led me to pursue a UX career. I have since had the opportunity to work on multiple projects as a UX consultant as well as volunteered for several organisations. My design experience covers the full end-to-end process but my strength lies within research, problem-solving, user journeys, IA to wireframing, prototyping and testing phases.

I am a positive self-starter and a true team player who thrives on collaborating with others. I firmly believe that working with a solid team will help achieve better results. I continually strive to master new skills and techniques in order to become a well-rounded designer. This has led me to recently study HTML and CSS to compliment my UX/UI skills where I already use Sketch, the Adobe CC Suite, InVision, Overflow, Figma, Principle and other design tools. These tools, along with my background in visual communication, allows me to create experiences which meet the expectations of both the client and users. To make sure I stay on top of UX trends and new techniques I also attend Meetups and Conferences on a regular basis.
