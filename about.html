About Me

Having worked as a Creative Project Lead for several companies in the past I have developed a mindset for the value of research and the iterative process. I believe that content development and storytelling is all about solving problems to create engaging experiences. This mentality and a passion for simple and flawless design has led me to pursue a career in UX design.

In the past year I have embraced this passion and embarked on getting a certificate in UX Design from Careerfoundry.

As a driven individual and someone who always strive to learn awhile staying up-to-date with new technology, I have also immersed myself in countless articles, books, Meet-ups and conferences to broadened my knowledge further. I am now hoping to show what I have to offer as I am currently looking for Junior UX Designer roles in London.
