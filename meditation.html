Meditation Coach

×

Healing Mind is a personal project where I set out to design a destination for a friend and spiritual coach to educate, share and connect with current practitioners and new clients. The webapp was created during a weekend design sprint and aimed to allow her clients to learn about and access meditations as well as book workshops.


Discovery

I met with the client to find out what her needs were.

  • Promote herself and a way for customers to reach her
  • Offer meditations and teachings
  • Ability to book events
  • Easy to use

I started by doing desk research on similar apps and services and developed a user map to reflect the clients’ thoughts. I used this as basis for further discussion.

Wireframing

In addition to speaking with the client I interviewed a group of her practitioners and created a series of low fidelity wireframes based on their feedback. Testing these with the client and users I learned that:

  • Daily Mindfulness practice would rarely be used and should therefore be removed.
  • As everyone prefer their meditation practice differently I’d need to add ability to personalise playback.
  • Player had redundant options for a meditation app and should be simplified.
  • The ‘Appearances’ consisted of a page per event which was redundant as bookings would be made through Eventbrite anyway.
×

Design

With new learnings I set about creating high fidelity wireframes. In order to do so I created and decided upon a series of assets including colour palette, logo, images, icons and fonts.

I created the client’s logo for a cleaner, clearer and more personal design. It’s made up of 3 parts; illustration, title and signature, which can be used separately if needed.

×

Prototype

I created a high fidelity prototype using Principle. It included screens for sign up, onboarding, meditation player, about page and events page.
